About me

January 7th, 2001 · Bandeirantes, Paraná, Brazil

I am currently a student of Computer Science at the State University of Northern Paraná and I am looking for my first opportunity as a freelancer or with a fixed job for a company as a Web developer or Frontend Developer. Since I was younger I started to work with computers and I developed an increasing interest in knowing how programs were made, what fundamentals and requirements to develop them. At the age of 14 I started to arouse interest in editing videos and images, since then, I started to research more deeply and specialize in design, learning about programming was the next step, because my goal was to unite both. When I entered the university in 2018, I decided to focus on the applications frontend, and for that I started studies in HTML, CSS and JavaScript, thus acquiring the necessary requirements to work in the area. Currently I work as a designer for companies, making promotional videos, flyers, banners and other projects, all involving editing.
